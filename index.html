ABOUT

Wedding Bootcamp Is A 3-Day Physical Event Of Uninterrupted In-Detail Sessions With Personal Attention, Comprising How To Ideate, Establish, Amplify And Run Your Wedding Photography And Filmmaking Business That Delivers The Best Artistic And Creative Shots. The 12 Masters From All Over India Will Come Together To Imbibe In-Depth Knowledge In Their Field Of Specialisation. You Will Learn The Creative Aspects, Business Model, Proven Strategies, Various Techniques And Methods To Build A Profitable Business In The Wedding Industry. In All, You Will Dive Into All The Artistic Aspects Of Wedding Photography And Filmmaking And The Business Aspects As A Business Owner. The First Edition Of Bootcamp Is Going To Take Place In Mumbai, India, From 16th To 18th September 2022.
